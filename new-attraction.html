Scenic Mountain Views

Experience breathtaking panoramic views of the Virginia Mountains from numerous lookout points and scenic drives. The Blue Ridge Parkway offers some of the most stunning vistas in the eastern United States, with countless opportunities to pull over and take in the majestic landscape.

From mountain peaks to rolling valleys, the changing seasons bring different but equally spectacular color palettes to these vistas. Photographers will find endless inspiration while outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy the many hiking trails leading to scenic overlooks.

Hiking Adventures

Lace up your boots and hit the trails! The Virginia Mountains offer hiking experiences for all skill levels, from gentle nature walks to challenging summit trails. The Appalachian Trail passes through this region, providing access to one of America's most famous long-distance hiking routes.

Local favorites include McAfee Knob, Dragon's Tooth, and Tinker Cliffs, known collectively as the "Triple Crown" of Virginia hiking. These moderate to difficult trails reward hikers with unforgettable views and photo opportunities.
